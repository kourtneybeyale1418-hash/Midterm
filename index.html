Frequently Asked Questions ‚ùì

We are open from 7:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday. Drop-off begins at 7:00 AM and pickup ends at 6:00 PM.

Please bring: extra clothes (2-3 complete outfits), diapers and wipes, comfort items (blanket/stuffed animal), and a family photo for our classroom board.

You'll receive a daily sheet detailing meals, naps, and activities. Weekly newsletters are sent every Friday with classroom highlights and upcoming events.

Please inform us of any allergies immediately. All food is screened for safety, and we follow age-appropriate nutrition guidelines.

Children rest from 12:00 PM to 2:00 PM. We create a calm environment with soft music and individual mats. Please bring a comfort item to help.

We understand separation anxiety is normal! We recommend quick, cheerful goodbyes and maintaining consistent drop-off routines. We'll keep you updated on your child's adjustment.
